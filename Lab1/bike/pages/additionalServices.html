Services

Customization

One of the main directions of our company is car tuning. In addition, we could not ignore such a topic as creating custom motorcycles. Therefore, we assemble custom motorcycles of any complexity. Our tuning workshop has all the equipment you need. In order to make an exclusive and beautiful project, you just need to contact us for advice. After that, our experienced masters of their craft will tell and show you which options are right for you. We value and love high-quality and high-tech things, so we assemble absolutely functional custom motorcycles that are comfortable for everyday use.

Evacuation

Tow truck special equipment equipped and equipped with fixing devices in the vertical position of the motorcycle on the platform of the tow truck. Motorcycles are very popular, as they allow you to enjoy fast driving and move comfortably in city traffic. However, there is the difficulty of transporting such vehicles in the event of an accident or technical malfunctions. Such problems are solved in the Moto Garage car assistance service, a motorized tow truck will be sent as soon as possible, and the motor vehicles will be transported to the place of repair.
