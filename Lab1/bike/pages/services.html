Services

Moto Selection

Professional selection of a motorcycle according to the specified criteria. Inspection, diagnostics, delivery, registration. Significant reduction in purchase price!

We will be happy to assist in choosing and buying the first motorcycle, or simply in a situation where there is no confidence in the seller's honesty. We carry out the selection throughout the territory of Belarus and the near abroad.

Diagnostic

Throttle workshop performs diagnostics of malfunctions of the main motorcycle systems in Minsk. We offer diagnostics of motorcycle engine operation, diagnostics of motorcycle brake system, diagnostics of suspension and other systems. We also offer motorcycle diagnostics after an accident, after falls and after buying a motorcycle.


Storage

At Moto Garage, we use shipping containers to store our customers' motorcycles. The robust and practical design of the containers allows not only transporting goods and property around the world, but also using them as a permanent storage location.

Maintenance

We carry out all types of work related to the maintenance of motor vehicles.
